# KAIZEN
**Your Ultimate Productivity & Personal Growth Suite**

---

## 🚀 Executive Summary

**Kaizen** is a premium, cross-platform productivity suite designed to help users achieve their goals through continuous, incremental improvement. Built with high-fidelity aesthetics and a minimalist approach, Kaizen unifies task management, habit tracking, interactive note-taking, and time visualization into a single, seamless experience.

Whether you're organizing your daily tasks, visualizing long-term goals, or brainstorming complex ideas, Kaizen provides the tools you need without the clutter.

## ✨ Core Value Proposition

- **Unified Workflow**: No more switching between separate apps for notes, tasks, habits, and calendars.
- **Visual Intelligence**: Understand your time and ideas better through interactive visual tools like the Box Clock and the Node-linking Canvas.
- **Uncompromised Design**: A beautiful, minimalist interface that feels native and responsive across all platforms, enhanced by dynamic themes.

---

## 🛠️ Key Features

### 1. The Box Clock 🧊
A revolutionary way to visualize your year and track your goals. 
- **Minimalist Legend**: Clean, glanceable indicators for daily and yearly progress.
- **Goal Tracking**: Visually maps out goals over calendar blocks, ensuring you stay aligned with your long-term vision.

### 2. Obsidian-Inspired Interactive Canvas 🌌
A spatial workspace for your mind.
- **Node-Linking System**: Connect ideas, notes, and media dynamically on a visually stunning dotted-grid background.
- **Rich Media Support**: Seamlessly render images with dynamic scaling and persistent title-visibility toggles.
- **Unbounded Creativity**: Drag, drop, and link nodes to create comprehensive mind maps and visual boards.

### 3. Comprehensive Productivity Dashboard 📊
Your daily command center.
- **Task Board**: Intuitive, Kanban-style board for managing projects and daily tasks.
- **Habit Tracker**: Build and maintain positive routines with integrated habit tracking.
- **Calendar**: A centralized view of your schedule, directly tied into your tasks and Box Clock goals.

### 4. High-Fidelity User Experience 🎨
Crafted for a premium feel on both Desktop and Mobile.
- **Dynamic Theming**: Choose from a variety of curated, beautiful themes (Light, Dark, Cherry Blossom, Coffee, Ember) that automatically adapt your app icon and window styling.
- **Desktop-Optimized**: Features a system-wide unified mouse feedback experience, custom window frame management, and borderless fullscreen execution.

---

## 💻 Technical Excellence

Kaizen isn't just beautifully designed; it's engineered for performance and reliability.

- **Cross-Platform**: Built with **Flutter**, Kaizen runs natively on Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android, and the Web.
- **Robust State Management**: Powered by **Riverpod** for reactive, fast, and scalable state management.
- **Cloud & Local Persistence**: 
  - Reliable offline support via `SharedPreferences` and local JSON serialization.
  - Seamless cloud synchronization and backend infrastructure powered by **Supabase**.
- **Performance Optimized**: Carefully optimized rendering, especially in complex views like the Interactive Canvas.
